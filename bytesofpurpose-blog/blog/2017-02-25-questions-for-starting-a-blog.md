Before you spin up a blog, it helps to settle a handful of questions first. Here is a five-step set to work through, from why you're doing it to how you'll get it in front of people. Answer these and the rest gets a lot easier.

<!-- truncate -->

## Step 1: Why?

- Why do I want to start a blog?
- What purpose will it serve?
- What will I gain out of it?
- What is my vision for it?
- How long do I plan on maintaining it?

## Step 2: The name

- Now that you have a vision, what name captures it?

## Step 3: What content, and how often?

- Will you be consistently writing posts for the blog? If so, how often?
- Commit yourself to creating a new post every so often:
    - Every time your notebook fills up
    - Every time you have an idea worth sharing
    - Every time some chosen time frame goes by without a post

## Step 4: What technology?

- Will you be using a public blog hosting site?
- Will you run your own server?
- Will you run your blog on the cloud?
- Will you create your own UI for the blog?
- Will you create your own backend for the blog?
- What do you plan on embedding into your blog?

## Step 5: How will you market it?

- How will you make others aware of your blog?
    - Will you put it on your resume?
    - Will you tell your friends about it?
    - Will you use catchy titles?
    - Will you find questions people are asking and reference it in answers?
