Your emotions are signals, and they are also fuel. The skill is learning which feelings to lean into, which to overcome, and how to stay steady when frustration, fear, or hopelessness show up. The questions below are meant to be sat with, not skimmed. Pick the cluster that matches what you're feeling and let it interrogate you.
