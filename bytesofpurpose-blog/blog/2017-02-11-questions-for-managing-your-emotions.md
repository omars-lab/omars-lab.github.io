Your emotions are signals, and they are also fuel. The skill is learning which feelings to lean into, which to overcome, and how to stay steady when frustration, fear, or hopelessness show up. The questions below are meant to be sat with, not skimmed. Pick the cluster that matches what you're feeling and let it interrogate you.

<!-- truncate -->

## Understanding your emotions

- How should I manage my feelings?
- What feelings am I experiencing right now?
- What triggered this emotional response?
- What role do emotions play in my life?
- What role do emotions play in my prayers?

## Directing your emotions (fuel vs. overcome)

- What feelings should I fuel and allow to push me?
- What feelings should I overcome?
- How will I use my feelings to drive me to reach my full potential?
- What feelings do I REFUSE to endure once more?

## Controlling your emotions

- How do I influence and control my own emotions?
- How do I overcome my sorrow?

## When you feel frustrated

- What frustrates me?
- What makes me feel down?
- What de-motivates me?
- How do I overcome these feelings?

## When you feel like giving up

- Is there any hope in this situation?
- Do I still believe in the value of what I'm doing?
- What would make me want to continue?

## When you feel like speaking out

- Should I speak up about this?
- What is the constructive way to express this feeling?
- Will speaking out serve a positive purpose?

## Conducting others' emotions

- What is your music that points others to the divine?
- What emotional journey will you take your co-workers through? What journey will you take yourself through?
- Whose emotions will you touch? How?
- How do you mold the emotions of others?

## Overcoming fear

> You are given an opportunity. You can deny it because you're afraid, afraid that you're too young, afraid of the unknown. Or you can take it. This is your choice.

- Do you live in fear every single day? Are you progressing?
- What are you afraid to do? Are you afraid to get started?

## Overcoming negative emotions

- What negative emotions am I minimizing on a daily basis?
- How often do I feel negative emotions, and how am I tracking this?
- What are the negative emotions that I feel?
- How do I overcome each of the negative emotions I encounter? What is my strategy for overcoming each one?

### Anger

- When do I feel anger: at someone, at someone who wronged me, at someone who annoyed me?
- How often do I get angry? (Try counting it.)

### Regret

- When do I feel regret: that I did something wrong, or that I should have done something differently?

### Hopelessness

- When do I feel hopeless: that something I want won't happen, or that something is in the way?

### Under-productivity

- When do I feel like I'm not doing enough? What feelings come from a lack of action?
- Not enough work, learning, or exercise? Not enough worship or focus?

### Lack of focus

- How often do I lose focus in prayer? (Try counting it.)
- Do I lose track of where I am, unsure how many rakas I've prayed?

## Tracking your emotions

- Which negative emotions should I start tracking, knowing I shouldn't feel them?
- What are the THREE things I should track, ranked by importance, with regard to the negative feelings I want to minimize in my life?

## Staying optimistic

### Purging negative thoughts

- What negative thoughts are polluting your mind: about yourself, about God, about love, about success, about where you are now, about your abilities?
- What negative thoughts will you no longer allow to infiltrate your mind?

### Maximizing positive emotions

- How do I maximize positive emotions?
- How do I leverage empowering words?
