Capturing every idea is the easy part. The hard part is deciding what to do *now* from a pile of initiatives, projects, and things you want to learn. These are the questions I ask myself to focus, weigh trade-offs, deprioritize what no longer matters, and lay it all out on a roadmap. Read them slowly and answer the ones that snag on something.

<!-- truncate -->

## Current focus

- What question(s) am I focused on answering now?
- What initiatives have I decided to work on now?
- What projects should I work on now?
- What things should I learn now?
- What's the most valuable thing I can do now?
- What do I **need** to be working on right now?

## How I prioritize

- How do I prioritize my initiatives?
- How do I determine what I should and shouldn't do?
- How do I decide what is important?
- On what basis do I decide to work on one thing over another?
- What goals do each of the priorities at hand help me attain?
- How are my desires influencing the goals I focus on?
- What do I feel like doing right now?
- When do I actually decide to work on something?
- What do my candidates depend on, and what are the ultimate goal(s) I'm prioritizing against?
- How will I sort my learning objectives/initiatives?
- Which of my learning objectives/initiatives do I NEED to learn vs WANT to learn?
- Which ones have I recently started?

## Prioritization factors

- What factors should I consider when prioritizing?
- What must I consider when prioritizing?
- How do the factors compare? Which factor is more important than the others?
- How do I determine this?
- Which one is easier to do?
- Which one needs to be done now?
- Which ones am I most interested in doing?
- How often should I address each of these concerns?
- What's the difference between a factor and a concern?

Principles:

- A task is higher in priority if it overlaps with many of my goals.
- A task is higher in priority if it needs to be done immediately and is important.

## By life category

- What do I need to be doing now to advance my career?
- What do I need to do now to become more organized?
- What do I need to immediately learn?
- What have I decided to do now to grow?
- What do we need to be doing now to advance our family?
- What have I decided to do now to better market myself?
- What projects do I need to work on now?

## Balancing priorities

- How do I balance learnings and projects?
- What can I do now vs what do I need to learn to do new things?

## What gets prioritized

Before prioritizing, it helps to know what's even in the running and where it lives.

- What goes on the roadmap, and what goes straight to my task manager?
- What do I prioritize, and what don't I prioritize?
- What are the things I am prioritizing, and where are they found?
- Are these in Jira? Are they scattered across roles?
- What initiatives am I prioritizing?

## Prioritizing projects

- How do I figure out what project(s) I should work on?
- How many of the following does it check off?
  - Will this project help me learn any of the things I want to learn?
  - Does this project overlap with the things I enjoy doing?
  - Do I want to work on this project?
- How marketable is this project?
- How distributable is this project?
- What are all of the other things I should consider when prioritizing projects?
- What projects am I passionate about?
- Of all the things I want to do, what should I do first?

I generally work on projects for one of two reasons: to learn something new, or to save myself time.

### Aligning projects to objectives

- What projects help me advance specific objectives?
- What objectives does each project help me meet?
- Why should I work on specific projects?
- What is the result of working on each project?
- What will each project teach me?
- What are the different objectives I want to advance through projects?
- What projects can advance my self-marketing objective?
  - Are any of the projects in a repo I can share?
  - Are there specific projects that will help me learn the things I want to learn?

### Projects reflecting priorities

- How do my priorities affect the projects I actually work on?
- Do my projects reflect my priorities?
- Am I working on projects that push me to explore the things I want to do and learn?
- How do the things I work on align with what I want to learn?
- Can I commit to a personal project where I apply the things I learn?

### Evaluating projects

- How will I evaluate each project?
- Why do I want to work on each of these projects?
- What are the benefits of working on each of the projects?
- Which of the things do I want to work on the most?
- How much does each project align with my interests?
- How do the projects I am working on overlap with what I want to learn?
- How do the projects I work on reflect how I market myself?
- What does each project showcase about the skills I have, my technical capabilities, my product design capabilities?

## Deprioritizing

- What should I stop doing?
- What should I deprioritize?
- What am I spending time on that isn't important?
- What are the things I wanted to do at some point that I no longer want to do?

## Constructing a roadmap

- Can I see my goals over time, on a timeline?
- Can I classify my goals into short-term and long-term?
- Can I see timelines for each different type of goal?
- What goals of mine are long term vs short term?
- What are my desired end-goals, by end of year, next year?
- Where do I see myself in 30 years, 20 years, 10 years?
- What is my vision for myself? Where do I want my goals to take me?
- How and where do all of my goals, milestones, etc. intersect?
- What goals are more important to complete than others?
- Given dependencies between goals, which should be prioritized first to attain them all in a timely fashion?
- Which activities should I focus on to collectively attain my goals?

## Tracking dependencies

- What goals depend on other goals/activities?
- What obstacles are hindering me from accomplishing my goals?

## Retrospecting

- How well did my prioritization work?
- What did I prioritize that I shouldn't have?
- What did I miss?
- How can I get better at prioritizing?
- What shortcomings have I observed?
