This is where I keep the inputs I have **deliberately chosen to let shape how I think**. Not ideas
that merely occurred to me (those are [Thoughts](/thoughts)), but the ones I curated in and keep on
purpose: the quotes that moved me, the affirmations I return to, the principles I live by. Less a
record of what crossed my mind than a record of what I let define it. (The *questions* I ask have
grown into their own collection: [Questions](/questions).)

<!-- truncate -->

## Mindset is what I curate, not what occurs to me

A thought and a mindset post can start from the same material; the difference is **deliberate
curation**:

- *Noticing* a quote is a passing thought. *Keeping* it, returning to it, letting it move me, is
  Mindset.
- An *observation* that crossed my mind is a Thought. The one I *adopt as a rule I live by* is
  Mindset.
- A line of advice I heard once is a thought. The affirmation I repeat to myself on hard days is
  Mindset.

A thought graduates INTO my mindset the moment I deliberately keep it to shape how I think (one of
[three ways a thought can graduate](/thoughts/about-my-thoughts): act on it → an Initiative, keep it
→ Mindset, distill it → Craft).
