# 📖 Glossary

This is the one place that gathers every term of art used across the site. The definitions
themselves live in the [Terminology](/handbook/terminology/building-software) pages, grouped by
subject; this page is the index that ties them together, so an unfamiliar word is never more than
one click from its meaning. (For how the whole site is organized, see the [Legend](/handbook).)

## The terminology by subject

- **[Architecture terms](/handbook/terminology/building-software/architecture)**: systems, services,
  applications, libraries, packages, and the build-time vs runtime distinctions.
- **[Planning & lifecycle terms](/handbook/terminology/getting-things-done/planning-and-lifecycle)**:
  ideas, initiatives, projects, goals, and priorities, and how they flow into one another.
- **[Work unit terms](/handbook/terminology/getting-things-done/work-units)**: roles, skills,
  activities, and workflows.
- **[Project management terms](/handbook/terminology/getting-things-done/project-management)**:
  planning, organizing, managing, and distilling.
- **[Company & career terms](/handbook/terminology/companies-and-career)**: roles, levels,
  competencies, and culture.
