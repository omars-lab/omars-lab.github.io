# Spacing and Elevation

Layout leans on a **soft 4px-based scale** with generous whitespace. Corners are gentle, shadows
are quiet, and motion is short and eased. The specimens below are sized directly by the tokens, so
the proportions are the real thing.

## Spacing scale

Nine steps, `--space-1` through `--space-9`. Each bar's width IS the token, so you can see the
proportion each step buys. Use these for **layout spacing** (margins, padding, gaps), not for font
sizes.

<SpacingScale />

## Radii

Corners are soft. Use `--radius-md` on genuine cards, `--radius-lg` on hero and chooser cards, and
`--radius-pill` / `--radius-full` for pills and circles.

<RadiusSwatches />

## Elevation

A quiet **two-step** shadow system, cool-ink tinted. A card rests on the faint `--shadow-sm` and
earns the medium `--shadow-md` plus a gentle lift **on hover**, not at rest. Hover the card to see
it (the lift holds still under reduced-motion).

`--shadow-sm` / `--shadow-md` equal the site's existing Infima tokens (`--ifm-global-shadow-lw` /

independent shadow scale.
:::

## Motion

Transitions are short and eased. Everything that moves **respects `prefers-reduced-motion`**, with
no exceptions; the hover-lift demo above holds its scene when reduced-motion is on.
