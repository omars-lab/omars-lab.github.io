# Colors

A deep **forest green** carries almost all the weight. Surfaces are a cool, muted off-white
"paper", deliberately not warm cream and not stark white. Ink is a near-black with a cool-gray
secondary. The **tea-party pastels** are sparing accent fills, and a **premium gold** family marks
locked or premium surfaces. Every swatch below is filled from the live token, so it is exactly
what ships (toggle light and dark in the navbar to see the palette shift).

## Brand green

The hero color. `--ifm-color-primary` (`#3C7256`) is the AA-safe green for **text and links**;
`--brand-green` (`#448061`) is the deeper "hero" green for large or decorative **fills**, where
text-contrast rules do not apply. The dark and light steps come from Infima's shade math.

## Tea-party pastels

:::caution Accent fills only, never text
The pastels are **backgrounds** for chips, pills, and tag fills. Never set a text `color` to a
pastel. The only ink allowed to ride on a pastel fill is **`--tea-ink`** (a deep green that meets
AA on all three).
:::

### Hairlines and dividers

Cards and dividers use a single 1px hairline. A repo-reality note: the design system *intends* the
hairline as `#DDE2DF`, but the site has not overridden Infima's default, so hairlines currently
render **`#EBEDF0`** (a cooler, lighter gray). Adopting `#DDE2DF` everywhere is a deliberate pending
decision, because it shifts every bordered surface. Use the token, not a raw hex.
