# 🧠 Knowledge

This is where I keep **what I know and how I understand it**: the durable **mental models** I reason
with, pulled up out of the individual topics into one place so I can return to them as a body.

A mental model here is not a dated log of something I did. It is the lasting *understanding* of a
domain, the vocabulary and the framing that stay useful across projects. That is why Knowledge is its
own root, alongside [Craft](/craft) (how I see the world) and [Journey](/journey) (how I see myself).

## What's here

- **[AI](/knowledge/ai)**: getting oriented in the GenAI domain, the fundamentals of agentic
  systems, the framework landscape, and how to ramp up.
- **[Career](/knowledge/career)**: how companies and roles work, career levels, cultural values, and
  the skills that get evaluated.
- **[Algorithms](/knowledge/algorithms)**: the mental models for the core data structures and
  algorithms (lists, trees, graphs, heaps, dynamic programming).
- **[Interviewing](/knowledge/interviewing)**: understanding the interview process and what
  companies expect.
