# 🪞 Self

**The inward journey — toward fulfilling my full potential.**

This half is *introspective*: it looks inward. It's the record of my pursuit of
mastery over *myself* — the faith, habits, and practices behind growing into the
person I want to become. I write it mostly for me, but I keep it public in case
watching one person's inward work is useful to yours.

> The companion half, [🛠️ Craft](/craft), turns the other way — outward.

## Browse the self

- **🕌 Faith** — the practices, apps, and automations behind growing spiritually.
- **🌱 Personal Growth** — the habits behind becoming who I want to be: reflecting,
