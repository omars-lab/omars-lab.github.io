## Questions

- What kind of businesses do I envision myself starting?
- How do I research and validate business ideas?
- What are the steps to open a business?
- How do I conduct marketing research for business ideas?
- What are the health and safety implications of different business approaches?
- How do I categorize and prioritize business ideas?

## Decision

Entrepreneurship is essential for creating value, pursuing independence, and building something meaningful. By systematically developing business ideas, conducting marketing research, and understanding the steps to start a business, I can transform entrepreneurial intentions into concrete business ventures. This habit enables me to pursue business opportunities with structured approaches and informed decision-making.

## Strategy

I strategically approach entrepreneurship by:
- Developing and categorizing business ideas by tier and feasibility
- Conducting marketing research and SEO trend analysis
- Understanding the practical steps required to start a business
- Researching health, safety, and operational implications of business approaches
- Creating systematic approaches to business idea validation and development
