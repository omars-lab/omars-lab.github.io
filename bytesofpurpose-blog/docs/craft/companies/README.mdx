# 🏢 Companies

How companies work from an engineer's perspective, the roles you grow through, the
skills that get evaluated, and the cultures that shape how teams operate.

## What's here

- **[Mental Models](/knowledge/career)**: how to think about companies from
  the inside:
  - *Career Levels*: how engineering levels differ in scope and expectation, and what
    traits define senior+ roles like staff engineer.
  - *Cultural Values*: how company culture varies (by size and values), with a close
    look at real examples.
  - *Skills*: the technical, leadership, and soft skills companies actually evaluate.
- **Skills**: practical frameworks for the soft skills the job demands: navigating
  ambiguity, managing complexity, and handling challenges.

*For: engineers navigating their career, anyone preparing to interview (see also
[Interview Prep](/craft/interview-prep)), and the curious about how tech companies work.*
