# 🎯 Interview Prep

Everything for the getting-hired journey, from the fundamentals you'll be tested on
to how the process works and what companies look for at each level.

## What's here

- **Understanding What Companies Expect**: before you prep, know the target: how
  levels and competencies differ, and which skills companies actually evaluate.
- **Understanding the Interview Process**: the overall flow and what to expect.
- **Preparing for Interviews**: behavioral, technical, system-design, and coding-round
  prep, plus building a personal story bank and questions to ask interviewers.
- **Data Structures & Algorithms**: the core DSA mental models (lists, trees, graphs,
  heaps, dynamic programming).
- **Coding Challenges**: problem-solving patterns and worked solutions.

*For: job-seekers and anyone leveling up toward the next role.*
