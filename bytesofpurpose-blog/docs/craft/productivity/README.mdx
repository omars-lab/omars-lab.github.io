# ⚡ Productivity

How I organize work and effort to get more of the right things done.

The **practices** themselves, organizing, discovering, analyzing, automating, planning,
prioritizing, tracking, reviewing, execution, and ideation, now live as habits on the
[Habits](/habits) resource (under [Professional](/habits/professional)). This topic keeps the
tools and artifacts that support them.

## What's here

- **Dashboards & Apps**: productivity trackers and apps I've built (kanban boards,
  initiative calendars, habit and metric dashboards).
- **Prompts**: the AI prompts I use to keep my workflow moving (daily todo carry-over,
  kanban customization).

*For: anyone who wants to organize their work more deliberately and build their own
productivity systems.*
