# 💻 Craft

**The outward journey, toward mastery of my craft.**

This space is *outrospective*: it looks out at the world. It's the working notebook
from my pursuit of mastery in the things I build and practice, what I've learned,
the techniques I lean on, the systems I've shipped. I keep it in the open because the
learnings are reusable: if they helped me get better, they might help you too.

> The companion space, [🛣️ Journey](/journey), turns the other way, inward.

## Browse the craft

- **🤖 Generative AI**: mental models and workflows for building with LLMs.
- **🧩 Software Development**: backend, frontend, scripting, plugins, and workspace craft.
- **📋 Product Management**: the idea→ship lifecycle: ideas, projects, experiments, roadmaps.
- **⚡ Productivity**: organizing, automating, and reviewing the way I work.
- **✍️ Blogging**: how this site is authored, diagrammed, and shipped.
- **🎯 Interview Prep**: coding challenges, mental models, and how to prepare.
- **🏢 Companies**: how companies evaluate, level, and value engineers.
- **🚀 Entrepreneurship**: learning the business side of building things.
