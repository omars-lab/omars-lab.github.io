# 🪞 Self Reflection

This is the durable side of self-questioning: not any one question, but the *practice* of
turning toward myself honestly and asking the hard thing. The lasting habit lives here; the
specific question sets I actually run are dated posts on the Initiatives blog.

## The practice

A good question asked of yourself does one job: it makes the thing you have been avoiding
hard to keep avoiding. Over the years I have collected the questions that did that for me and
grouped them by what they are for, finding purpose, facing fears, deciding what to learn,
measuring progress. The keystone post explains how I run the practice and what the little
power icons on each question mean:

- **[What I Ask Myself](/legend/what-i-ask-myself)**: the practice, and the legend for
  the power icons.

The individual question sets are temporal, so they live as posts on the Initiatives blog,
