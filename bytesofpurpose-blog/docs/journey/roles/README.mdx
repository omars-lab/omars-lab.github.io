# 🎭 Roles

**The parts I play, and who I become when I play them.**

I find it useful to think of myself not as one undifferentiated person but as a cast of **roles**.
When I sit down to measure and analyze my own life, I am playing *the Analyzer*. When I keep things
running, *the Maintainer*. Each role is a lens: it comes with its own set of skills I lean on, the
kinds of artifacts I tend to produce, the habits that keep it alive, and a reason it earns a place in
my life.

This section catalogs those roles. Each page is a durable portrait of one role, not a dated log of
something I did. It answers the same questions every time:

- **Why the role matters** to who I am becoming.
- **The skills** I use when I am in it.
- **The artifacts** I produce while playing it.
- **The habits** that keep the role alive.

The roles are sourced from a private, evolving knowledge base where I work out what each one really
is. What lands here is the shareable portrait: the transferable half, with the private application
left behind.

> The companion spaces: [🕌 Faith](/faith), [⚡ Productivity](/productivity), and the rest of the
> [🛣️ Journey](/journey) turn inward on *why* I build. Roles turn a lens on *who does the building*.
