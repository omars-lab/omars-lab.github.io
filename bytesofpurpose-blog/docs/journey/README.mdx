# 🛣️ Journey

**What drives me forward on this journey.**

This space is *introspective*: it looks inward. I have to be inward-facing, aware, and conscious to
catch what actually moves me, the motivations and the pieces of the puzzle behind *why* I build. So
this is the record of that noticing: my faith, the drive to grow, the business I want to build, the
way I organize my effort toward what matters. The inward awareness is the method; catching what
drives me forward is the point.

I write it mostly for me, but I keep it public in case watching one person work out their own "why"
is useful to yours.

> The companion space, [💻 Craft](/craft), turns the other way: outward, toward *how* I do the work.

## Browse the journey

- **🕌 Faith**: the practices, apps, and automations behind growing spiritually.
- **🌱 Personal Growth**: the habits behind becoming who I want to be: reflecting, reading,
  mentorship, health, and finances.
- **🚀 Entrepreneurship**: the pull toward building a business, and what I am learning going from
  engineer to founder.
- **⚡ Productivity**: how I organize my effort toward what matters, the dashboards, apps, and prompts
  that keep me moving.
