# ⚡ Productivity

How I organize work and effort to get more of the right things done.

## What's here

- **Processes** — my execution, ideation, and interviewing processes.
- **Organizing / Discovering / Analyzing / Automating** — techniques for structuring,
  finding, measuring, and automating work.
- **Tool Usage** — patterns for getting the most out of tools.
- **Dashboards & Apps** — productivity trackers and apps I've built (kanban boards,
  initiative calendars, habit and metric dashboards).
- **Vocabulary** — the CLI, development, and project-management terms I use.
- **Habits** — the productivity habits behind it (planning, prioritizing, tracking,
  reviewing, and more).

*For: anyone who wants to organize their work more deliberately and build their own
productivity systems.*
