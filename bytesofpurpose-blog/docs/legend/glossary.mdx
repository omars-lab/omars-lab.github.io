# 📖 Glossary

This is the one place that gathers every term of art used across the site. The definitions
themselves live in the [Terminology](/legend/terminology/software-development) pages, grouped by
topic; this page is the index that ties them together, so an unfamiliar word is never more than
one click from its meaning. (For how the whole site is organized, see the [Legend](/legend).)

## The glossaries by topic

- **[Software development terms](/legend/terminology/software-development)**: systems, services,
  libraries, packages, and the build-time vs runtime distinctions.
- **[Productivity: development terms](/legend/terminology/productivity/terminology-development)**:
  ideas, initiatives, projects, goals, and priorities, and how they flow into one another.
- **[Productivity: CLI terms](/legend/terminology/productivity/terminology-cli)**: roles, skills,
  activities, and workflows.
- **[Productivity: project-management terms](/legend/terminology/productivity/terminology-project-management)**:
  planning, organizing, managing, and distilling.
- **[Company terms](/legend/terminology/companies)**: roles, levels, competencies, and culture.
