# 🤖 Generative AI

Everything I'm learning and building in the generative-AI space — from the
fundamentals of the domain to designing production systems to my own day-to-day
workflow with LLMs.

  the fundamentals of agentic systems, a learning guide, and notes from the field

- **Building GenAI Systems** — how I approach designing and shipping GenAI systems:

  architectures, and taking a POC to production.
- **My GenAI Workflow** — how I personally use GenAI: my MCP setup, running LLMs
  locally, tinkering with RAG, and the LLM-powered initiatives I'm running.

*For: engineers learning GenAI, anyone designing an AI feature, and the curious who
want a grounded, build-first take on the space.*
