# 🕌 Faith

The intersection of my faith and the things I build — small tools and habits that
support worship and spiritual growth.

## What's here

- **Automations** — home automations that serve worship: an athan (call-to-prayer)
  player, a Qurʾan player, and tasbeeh (dhikr) tracking.
- **Apps & Trackers** — a prayer-tracking Apple Watch app and a *hifz* graph for
  tracking Qurʾan memorization.
- **Growing Spiritually** — the habits and practices behind consistent spiritual growth.

*For: Muslims who build, and anyone curious how faith and software can support each other.*
