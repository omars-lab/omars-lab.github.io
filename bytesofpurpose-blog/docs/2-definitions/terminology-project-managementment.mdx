# 🏛️ Planning vs Managing vs Organizing vs Distilling

The act of planning involves figuring out what to do / putting together a plan of action ... 
But that is in essence what I am trying to do across my different habits ... 
Thus I shouldn't have a planning folder ... but rather link to the "planning" oriented activities across the different buckets ...


With regards to priorities ... does one manage them ... plan them? Organize them ? 


* [Google: "does one plan priorities or manage them"](https://www.google.com/search?q=does+one+plan+priorities+or+manage+them)

> **Planning is about determining all of the tasks that you need to complete in order to achieve your goals**.
I am not just planning ... thus one would't "plan" priorities ... one needs to manage them ...

Same thing with habits ... I do need to plan what each habit involves ... but when I have a bunch of rabbits fighting for the same time, I need to manage them ...
