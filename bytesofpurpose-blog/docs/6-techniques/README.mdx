## Difference from Skills and Craftsmanship

**Techniques** vs **Skills** vs **Craftsmanship**:

- **Techniques** â†’ Specific methods and how-to guides for accomplishing tasks (e.g., "how to embed a diagram", "how to parse JSON")
- **Skills** â†’ Abilities and competencies you develop (e.g., problem-solving, system design, coding)
- **Craftsmanship** â†’ Tools, workflows, and processes for how you work (e.g., workspace setup, tool selection, workflow optimization)

Techniques are about **what to do** and **how to do it**, while skills are about **what you can do**, and craftsmanship is about **how you work**.
