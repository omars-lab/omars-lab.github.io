# 🔄 Habits

This is where I keep **how I habitually operate**: the routines and practices I return to, pulled
together from across my work and life into one place.

A habit here is not a one-off thing I did. It is a repeatable practice I lean on, the small things
that compound over time. That is why Habits is its own root, alongside [Craft](/craft) (how I see
the world) and [Journey](/journey) (how I see myself).

## What's here

- **[Professional](/habits/professional)**: the working habits, automating, organizing, planning,
  prioritizing, reviewing, and the craft-side routines (blogging, entrepreneurship).
- **[Personal](/habits/personal)**: the life habits, growing, learning, health, finances, reading,
  reflecting, and pursuing interests.
- **[Spiritual](/habits/spiritual)**: the faith practices that ground the rest.
