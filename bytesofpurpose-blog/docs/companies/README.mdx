# 🏢 Companies

How companies work from an engineer's perspective — the roles you grow through, the
skills that get evaluated, and the cultures that shape how teams operate.

## What's here

- **Roles & Levels** — how engineering levels differ in scope and expectation, and
  what traits define senior+ roles like staff engineer.
- **Culture** — how company culture varies (by size and values), with a close look at
  real examples.
- **Skills** — the technical, leadership, and soft skills companies actually evaluate,
  plus how to navigate ambiguity and complexity on the job.

*For: engineers navigating their career, anyone preparing to interview (see also
[Interview Prep](/docs/interview-prep)), and the curious about how tech companies work.*
