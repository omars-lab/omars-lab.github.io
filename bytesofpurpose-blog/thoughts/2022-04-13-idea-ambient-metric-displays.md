A metric I have to open an app to see is a metric I forget. I want the numbers I care about to
live in my ambient field of view, where a glance is enough.

<!-- truncate -->

**The idea.** Put a personal metric where I already look, two surfaces:

- **LaMetric motivator.** An app that surfaces a personal metric on the LaMetric display on my
  desk, so the number I am trying to move is always in sight.
- **NotePlan menu bar.** A NotePlan menu-bar app that shows the day's metrics at a glance, so the
  same numbers are one look away while I work.

Both are the same idea (ambient, glanceable metrics) on two displays, so they likely share the
metric source.

## Plan

- [ ] Decide the metric source the two displays read from.
- [ ] Build the LaMetric app that pushes a personal metric to the device.
- [ ] Build the NotePlan menu-bar app that shows the day's metrics at a glance.

## Success criteria

- The metric I am tracking is visible at a glance on at least one ambient display, no app to open.
