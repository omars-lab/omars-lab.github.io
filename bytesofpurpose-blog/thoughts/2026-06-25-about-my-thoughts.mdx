This is where I keep the **ideas that occurred to me** but I have not acted on yet. The raw mental
events: a thing I might build, a hypothetical I am playing out, a bet I am making, a critique that
came to mind. Not the dated things I actually did (those are [Initiatives](/initiatives)), not the
inputs I keep to shape how I think (those are [Mindset](/mindset)), and not the distilled lessons
that held up over time (those are [Craft](/craft)). Just the thoughts, as they occurred.

<!-- truncate -->

## A thought has three ways to graduate

A thought is a candidate, not a commitment. Where it ends up depends on what I do with it:

- If I **act on it**, it becomes a dated [Initiative](/initiatives).
- If I **deliberately keep it to shape how I think**, it becomes part of my [Mindset](/mindset).
- If it **distills into a lesson that keeps holding up**, it graduates into [Craft](/craft).

The difference is **occurrence vs. curation**. A quote that just *occurred* to me is a Thought;
*keeping* it because it moves me is [Mindset](/mindset). A question that crossed my mind is a
Thought; the deliberate *set* I ask, on purpose, becomes one of my [Questions](/questions). The
thought is the raw event; the curated thing it becomes is Mindset or Questions.
