Typography

Headings are Space Grotesk, a techy geometric grotesque that leans the brand toward its engineering identity; it takes a slightly tight negative tracking at display sizes. Body and UI are Geist at 16px and a generous 1.7 line-height for long-form comfort. Code is Geist Mono. Each specimen below renders in the real face, with the tokens that produced it shown underneath.

Display · Space Grotesk​

The headline voice. Large headlines take a hair of negative tracking for a crisp, engineered feel.

Purposeful code

Frauncessize --text-displayweight --weight-semiboldtracking --tracking-tight

Where faith meets craft

Frauncessize --text-h1weight --weight-semiboldtracking --tracking-heading

Purposeful code, one byte at a time.

Frauncessize --text-leadweight --weight-regulartracking --tracking-normal

✦ Our choice: Space Grotesk

Space Grotesk is now the display face. It leans the brand into its engineering identity: a geometric grotesque reads as tooling and craft, and it pairs cleanly with Geist body (both are modern sans faces, so the display and body feel of one family). The editorial serifs (Fraunces, the previous pick, plus Newsreader, Spectral, Lora) are warmer but read more personal-blog than engineering; the Didones (DM Serif, Instrument) feel fashion-masthead. Space Grotesk keeps the headlines crisp and a little technical, which is the register we want. It is wired through the type tokens as --font-serif-display (the token name is kept for continuity even though the face is no longer a serif).

Body sans · Geist​

The workhorse for UI and long-form prose. Set at 16px with a 1.7 line-height for comfortable reading.

Real-world engineering artifacts. Examples include sequence diagrams, roadmaps, code snippets, and automations. Short, concrete, verb-first.

Geistsize 16px / 1.7weight --weight-regulartracking --tracking-normal

Eyebrow and mono​

The eyebrow label is uppercase Geist, tracked out to 0.18em, in the primary green. The middot · is the brand's connective separator. Code is Geist Mono.

ENGINEERING · FAITH · CRAFT

Geistsize --text-eyebrowweight --weight-semiboldtracking --tracking-eyebrow

const purpose = 'one byte at a time';

Geist Monosize --text-body-mdweight --weight-regulartracking --tracking-normal
The eyebrow tracking is editorial-only

--tracking-eyebrow (0.18em) is for the hero and section eyebrow labels. Badges, pills, tags, and chips keep their own tighter micro-tracking; do not normalize those to 0.18em, it looks broken on a small chip.

The type scale​

Headings (Fraunces) and body (Geist) each walk a fixed scale. Reach for a scale step, not a raw rem.
