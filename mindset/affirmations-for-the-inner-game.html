Affirmations for the Inner Game

January 1, 2019 · 10 min read
Omar Eid
Principal AI Architect & Entrepreneur

For years I've kept a running list of affirmations: faith-based reminders, lines from motivational talks, and one-liners that snapped me out of a rut. Some are scripture-adjacent, some are pure hype, some are questions I ask myself. They were scattered across notebooks and notes apps until I gathered and grouped them by theme.

I'm sharing them here the way I use them: not as a system to follow, but as a deck to flip through when you need the right words. Read the section that matches where your head is. Take what resonates, leave the rest.

Faith & Trusting God​

Order my steps​

  • He is orchestrating your strength
  • He is giving you steps you are capable of handling
  • Hold his hand and start to climb to your potential
  • Order my steps Lord
  • I trust you
  • You have equipped me with everything I need right now
  • Your life will move in the direction of your words
  • What better words than his
  • The Names of God should drive your actions
  • I put in him all it takes to withstand the pressure
  • Walk by faith and not by sight
  • God just needs to will to do, you need to act. Will is not enough!

God has a plan​

  • GOD HAS SOMETHING IN PLAN FOR YOU
  • HE WON'T GIVE YOU SOMETHING YOU CAN'T TAKE
  • HE WON'T GIVE YOU A CHALLENGE YOU CAN'T HANDLE
  • HE IS BUILDING EVERYONE OF US
  • God is not preparing the blessing for you, God is preparing you for the blessings!
  • Any time you get anything too soon, you will trip on the steps!
  • What is God preparing you for?

God is with you​

  • GOD HAS ALWAYS BEEN THERE AND HE ALWAYS WILL
  • YOU DON'T HAVE TO SEE HIM TO KNOW THAT SOMEONE IS LOOKING OUT FOR YOU
  • God knows that you will be able to bear the burden he gave you, and that you will come out of it stronger, better!
  • God will not present you with something you can't handle!
  • Have you been trusted with some trouble?!
  • YOU NEVER PROMISE THE OBVIOUS, YOU ONLY PROMISE WHEN THERE IS A CAUSE FOR DOUBT. GOD PROMISES THAT HE WONT GIVE YOU SOMETHING YOU CAN HANDLE!

Pray to Him​

  • DO NOT ABUSE GOD'S MERCY!
  • THE PRESENCE OF A STORM DOES NOT INDICATE THE ABSENCE OF GOD
  • Why do you only beg Him when it starts to storm?!

Faith as a force​

  • Faith is the oil that takes the friction out of living
  • It all starts with belief
  • BELIEVE!
  • Faith finds a way!
  • I am going to show you how great I am! ~ Muhammad Ali
  • I work on making the people around me believe that we are going to be great.
  • Don't believe its a lack of funds that makes you unsuccessful, its a lack of belief!
  • HE IS ABLE WHO THINKS HE IS ABLE
  • I WILL
  • Change your garments!
  • Prepare for something that hasn't happened yet!

Strength, Pain & Endurance​

Let hardships build you​

  • Things don't happen by accident! I didn't stumble upon you by accident!
  • The steps of a good man are ordered by God!
  • Experiences build you. They give you faith.
  • Be thankful for experiences
  • Be thankful for goodbyes
  • When pain comes it's time to show your character
  • I never fail, I only have learning experiences!
  • NOTHING JUST HAPPENS!
  • Praise God in the middle of the pain, trust where He is taking you!
  • Your strength is perfected in the weak places of life!
  • I want you to praise me in the middle of the pain then I know you trust me that I am taking you somewhere!
  • Im sickening to talking to people who think that things just happen.

Deal with pain​

  • It's how you handle the pain!
  • I CHOOSE TO FIGHT BACK!
  • PAIN IS NOT PERMANENT!
  • PAIN IS TEMPORARY!
  • I can guarantee you, if you work through your pain, on the other side, there is a reward!
  • I'M HAVING A CHARACTER BUILDING DAY!

Learn from pain​

  • Our Pains Are Similar, Our Solutions Are Different!
  • Somebody can take the hand you've been dealt and be successful!
  • What Can I Do With The Stuff You Gave Me!

Be strong​

  • Be strong! And have the audacity to keep being strong!
  • When you are strong, nobody can shake you
  • She was strong enough to push through
  • If you bend you won't break
  • I was weak, I was vulnerable, I was hurting, I was in pain, I was at my worst, but I was still a giant!
  • I'M A GIANT! I'M A FREAK! WHEN I WANTED TO FOLD I DIDN'T FOLD!
  • YOU CAN'T MESS WITH A HURT GIANT!
  • YOUR PAIN WON'T STOP ME! IF YOU WANT ME TO STOP, YOU HAVE TO KILL ME!

Never quit​

  • THE UNSTOPPABLE MARCH
  • DON'T STOP TILL YOU REACH THE TOP!
  • IF YOU ARE WEAK YOU WILL QUIT!
  • YOU CANT AFFORD TO BE TIRED!
  • TOUGH TIMES DONT LAST FOREVER!
  • TOUGH TIMES DON'T LAST, TOUGH PEOPLE DO!
  • You cannot be a WAIVER!
  • Be relentless
  • IF YOU GET TIRED, LEARN TO REST, NOT QUIT!
  • When You Cannot, You Must!
  • IF YOU QUIT THIS TIME, YOU WILL QUIT AGAIN!
  • Quitters never win! Winners never quit!
  • They were ready to quit, but they didn't. Don't be a quitter!
  • Go where people believe you are unstoppable!

Discipline, Action & Drive​

Act now​

  • This Moment. This Moment. Now. Now. Now. Do You Get What I Am Saying!
  • You need to work out the muscle of doing!
  • Push forward and take action anyway!
  • Take consistent action everyday!
  • If I don't do this work, ... who will!
  • One Good Deed Leads To Another!
  • You Cant Live At All Unless You Live Fully Now. START!
  • ACTIONS EXPRESS PRIORITIES!
  • You have just got to make one more decision!
  • Ideation without execution is delusion!
  • Thinking is the first step towards a revolution!

Discipline yourself​

  • Discipline Yourself!
  • Your life will never change if you do not do the things you do not feel like doing!
  • What are your MUSTs?!
  • We don't get what we want, we get what we have to have!
  • What changes people is when their should's become musts!
  • What must you do?!

Drive and passion​

  • What drives you?!
  • Let Love Drive You!
  • Empires Fall... Love Survives!
  • The Real Revolution, The Revolution Of The Mind, The Loveolution
  • Purpose is your belief in motion!
  • DON'T PURSUE PROCESS. PURSUE PURPOSE!
  • PASSIONS AND PURPOSE ALWAYS PRODUCE PROFIT!
  • Its about history ... that never repeats itself!
  • Let your passion take you places!

Be thankful​

  • Trade what upsets you - the expectations that are not being met - with appreciation for what you have!

Risk, Failure & Imperfection​

Be ready to fail​

  • ADOPT A FAIL FIRST MENTALITY!
  • DO YOU HAVE THE GUTS TO FAIL?
  • IF YOU DON'T FAIL ... YOU ARE NOT TRYING!

Avoid perfection​

  • If you have everything under control, you are moving too slow!
  • ENGINEERS ARE TOO OBSESSED WITH PERFECTION TO DO ANYTHING!
  • THERE ARE STAGES TO A PRODUCT ...
  • YOU DON'T INVEST IN PERFECTING YOUR TECH UNTIL YOU ARE CONFIDENT IN YOUR TECH!

Focus & Self-Mastery​

Be focused​

  • You can't afford to be a wandering generality!
  • You must be a meaningful specific!
  • You have to really be committed to something to get everything you can out of it!
  • You can't do everything at once...

Focus on your strengths​

  • Are you doing things right or are you doing the right things?
  • Turn your strengths into superpowers ...

Be resourceful​

  • How resourceful are you?
  • What resources do you have?

Be mindful of your thoughts​

  • What thoughts do you allow into your mind?!
  • Pay attention to your inner dialog

Be mindful of your tongue​

  • Words are powerful!

Listen closely​

  • Listen to your intuition, the voice that tells you this is what you could do!

Confidence & Fearlessness​

Confidence through experience​

  • You have been through plenty!
  • You have seen enough!
  • EGO is the anesthesia that deadens the pain of stupidity
  • Pride is the burden of a foolish person

Self-belief​

  • Trust your ideas
  • You are lead!
  • Promote yourself

Accepting praise​

  • Humiliating yourself is not humble

Be fearless​

  • Fear finds you in different ways.
  • The Illusion of Security Robs Ambition!
  • You don't want to be secure, you want to be strong

Stand your ground​

  • WALK ALONE IF YOU HAVE TO!
  • Don't be mad at people who stop clapping
  • At some point in your life you will find yourself alone
  • Stand Your Ground

Identity, Purpose & Living Fully​

Mindset reminders​

  • It's important to sort through the things I want... as they partially capture WHY I DO what I do!
  • We don't complain on the things we can't change!
  • When will you stop eating dirt
  • Stop complaining about where you are!

Be unique​

  • THEIR CAN ONLY BE 1, AND YOU ARE IT!!
  • DONT LET THE WORLD TELL YOU WHO YOU ARE
  • You are different because of God! Because you need to do something for his name!
  • The fact that you are different is not an accident!
  • Everything that you are is for a reason!
  • You are the sum of all of your experiences, thoughts and skills
  • OWN YOUR STORY
  • DONT LET THE WORLD TELL YOU WHAT TO REGRET
  • If you don't like where you are from, you can never love where you are going
  • YOU DESERVE TO BE AROUND PEOPLE THAT PUSH YOU!

Life is temporary​

  • What dreams will die with you?
  • What talents will die with you?
  • How you living?
  • We either live our dreams or live our fears, which one are you living?
  • ALL HUMANS DIE BUT FEW HAVE LIVED!
  • LIFE IS EITHER A DARING ADVENTURE ... OR NOTHING!

Unleash yourself​

  • I CAN, I WILL, I MUST!
  • Free Yourself From The Shackles Of Your Mind!
  • A lot of people like to complain but they wont do anything about their situation.
  • YOU could have been free years ago!
  • The Real Battle Ground Is In Your Mind!
  • A New Outfit Doesn't Make New You A New Person!
  • A New Life Comes From A New Mind And A New Way Of Looking At Your Life
  • Its What You Say About You THAT Threatens Your Destiny!
  • YOU HAVE TO FIX THE MIND BEFORE YOU CAN ACCEPT A BLESSING!
  • If you don't change your attitude, you wont reach your altitude!
  • Exercise your mind!
  • Rethink your mountaintop!
  • I am going to do everything in my power to become a victor not a victim!

You matter​

  • You must realize who you are!
  • Decide you love your self enough!
  • Repeat after me: I am good enough!
  • We're So Busy Studying Other People We Fail To Study Ourselves
  • Do not Allow The Opinion Of Others To Control Your Future
  • You can't relive my experience!
  • You can't imitate my passion!
  • You can't replicate my will!
  • You can't live my vision!
  • You get in life what you deserve not what you want!
  • Treat Your Voice Like A Kite And The Wind Is The Song. - Pharrell Williams
  • Use your genius on your own behalf
  • Act on your own behalf!
  • Act on your dreams!
  • Turn your life into a work of art because anything less is a tragedy!
  • I haven't had my most creative moment yet!
  • DEEP DOWN IN YOUR HEART YOU KNOW YOU ARE NOT LIVING TO YOUR TRUE POTENTIAL!
  • What will you do before your body perishes?

Wisdom and notes​

  • Judge a tree by the fruit that it bears!
  • When you're poor, you have to dig inside for value!
  • The toughest battles come to the toughest warriors!
  • I am more than my mind! I have a heart and soul!
  • As I mature my thinking ... different quotes take on different meanings ...

A note on these​

These aren't original to me. They're collected over years from sermons, speeches, books, and the occasional 2 a.m. realization, then filed under the themes above. The point of keeping them isn't to memorize a creed. It's to have the right sentence within reach when the moment calls for it.
